About this Project:

This is an idea that I have for app. I'ts called SKIN. Our skin is one of the biggest organs in our body and sometimes we don't pay attention to what we use on it.

This app will help you to find personal care products and their level of toxity based on Enviromental Working Group's data base. With this information people will have the choice to decide if that shampoo or that cream is safe for them and their loved ones.
