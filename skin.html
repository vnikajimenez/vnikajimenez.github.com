Skin

Mobile Design, Logo Design, User experience & Layout

Skin is a very simple app that will help you find personal care products and their level of toxicity based on Enviromental Working Group's data base.

People are exposed by breathing in sprays and powders, swallowing chemicals on the lips or hands or absorbing them through the skin. Studies find evidence of health risks. Biomonitoring studies have found cosmetics ingredients like phthalate plasticizers, paraben preservatives, the pesticide triclosan, synthetic musks, and sunscreens as common pollutants in men, women and children.

With this information people will have the choice to decide if that shampoo or that body cream they are trying to buy at the supermarket is safe for them and/or their loved ones.
