About

Veronica Jimenez

Design Enthusiast

San Francisco, California

I've always been inspired by visual elements. Growing taking pictures up in a culture full of color and mysticism like Mexico is one of the things I've always been thankful for.

When I finished college instead of staying in my hometown and getting a job like most people do. I had the opportunity to travel for a couple of years, where I experienced new cultures, shapes and languages and this opened my mind in such a way that I realized that I wasn't passionate about business Management

So one thing took me to the another, and my passion for technology and telling histories drove me to this point.

Last year I started taking graphic design, Adobe Suite, user experience at San Francisco State University. I've also been learning Html, Css and Javascript with General Assembly at the Front End Web Development intensive program.

I'ts so refreshing for me to learn about the web world and how it works.

Im looking for a place where I can put my new skills to work in real life and hopefully in some time soon I will be able to call myself a web designer.
