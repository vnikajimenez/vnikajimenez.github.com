Single is an application for sending a shopping list to your smartphone. I wanted a lightweight way to create a list on my desktop and have it available from my phone while I was doing groceries. Unlike task management apps, I wanted this to feel like a disposable list, much like writing the list on a scrap of paper.


I started by going through a bunch of other task list productivity apps. Then I sketched out a simple user flows, and some wireframes. I ended up jumping right from wireframe into HTML and CSS to do the final visual design.


I really tried to keep this product as minimal as possible, while still prompting the user through the actions.


The product is now live but still has some issues when text is too long. I also never quite got the interaction feeling right for checking off the items from your phone. I tested it with a couple of friends and found a couple of people tried copying lists off of recipe websites and tried to paste them right into the input box. This would be a challenge we'd need to figure out to really make this product production ready.
